About Us

Refugee Protection International (RPI) is an NGO partner to SIMRO to improve the availability of quality maternal and child health services for conflict-affected civilians in Idlib Province, Syria. Together the project seeks to help address the lack of safe, highly equipped underground MCH centers and skilled staff.

SIMRO
PROJECTS

PROTECT initiative: evaluate the security of existing field health facilities in Syria, provide safe facilities for health workers and patients.

  • Avicenna Women and Children’s referral hospital in rebel-controlled territories (where health care system has been destroyed by the Assad regime), with multiple operating rooms and Telehealth communications.
  • Needs and Population Monitoring - 2015, in coordination with International Organization of Migration (IOM). Project goals: •Obtain population statistics •Monitor the movement of the displaced population inside Syria •Assess the needs of the population in the conflict zone in Syria
  • Setting up Health Information System (HIS) in the cities of Idleb, Hama, and Aleppo to connect all health facilities with NGOs and the UNOCHA (U.N. Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs) Health Cluster in Turkey
  • Setting up primary health care centers that deliver health services to Syrians in besieged areas in Syria

Avicenna Progress

Avicenna National Hospital, will be the largest underground hospital in Idlib. The Hospital is expected to be patient ready by Late 2018. Thanks to the hardworking team on the ground and our generous supporters, we have finished construction of the underground floors! We are now focusing on stocking the hospital with furniture and medical equipment.
Simulation equipment is arriving at Avicenna, this will allow Avicenna Providers to practice new surgical skills to improve their clinical knowledge and improve quality and safe patient care.
